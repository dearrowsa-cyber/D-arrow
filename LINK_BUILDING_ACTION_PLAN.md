# 🔗 Link Building Strategy & Action Plan - D Arrow Digital

## Executive Summary
Link building is one of the three most important Google ranking factors. This document provides a detailed action plan to acquire 10-15 high-quality backlinks quarterly for sustainable organic growth.

**Baseline:** Run the following to check current backlinks:

## 🎯 Link Building Goals

### Quarterly Targets:
- **10-15 high-quality backlinks** per quarter
- **Average DA (Domain Authority) > 30**
- **Diverse anchor text** (brand, keywords, generic)
- **Relevant to digital marketing industry**
- **Sustainable growth** (not spam/paid links schemes)

### Types of Links to Pursue:
1. **Editorial Links** (60%) - Earned through content/PR
2. **Resource/Directory Links** (30%) - Industry recognition
3. **Partnership Links** (10%) - Strategic relationships

## 📰 Press Release & Media Coverage

**Effort: Medium-High | Impact: Very High | Timeline: 4 weeks per release**

### Process:
1. **Identify newsworthy topic** (2 days):
   - New service launch
   - Major partnership
   - Awards/recognition
   - Industry milestone
   - Research findings

2. **Write press release** (3 days):
   - Include 2-3 backlink opportunities (your site + partner sites)
   - Use industry keywords
   - Target length: 400-500 words
   - Include quotes from leadership

3. **Distribute via PRWeb** (1 day):
   - Cost: $200-400 per release
   - Expected backlinks: 2-5 from news sites
   - Expected media coverage: 3-10 publications

## 📞 Outreach Strategy

### The Outreach Process:

1. **Prospecting** (30% of time)
   - Identify relevant blogs, websites, directories
   - Qualify based on:
     - Domain Authority (prefer DA > 20)
     - Relevance to digital marketing
     - Link value (dofollow preferred)
     - Editorial quality

2. **Research** (20% of time)
   - Find correct contact information
   - Read recent articles to customize pitch
   - Understand their content style
   - Tools: Hunter.io, RocketReach, LinkedIn

3. **Personalized Outreach** (40% of time)
   - Write personalized email (never template)
   - Reference their recent content
   - Explain specific value to their audience
   - Keep email short (< 100 words)
   - Include link to relevant resource

4. **Follow-up** (10% of time)
   - First follow-up: After 5-7 days
   - Second follow-up: After 12-14 days
   - Then move on (don't be pushy)

### Outreach Email Templates:

**Template 1: Resource Mention**
```
Hi [Name],

I came across your article on "[Article Title]" and really appreciated your 
take on [specific insight].

I recently published a [resource type] that expands on [one of your points]:
[Your URL]

I thought your readers might find it useful as a supplement to your article.

Best,
[Your Name]
D Arrow Digital
```

**Template 2: Collaboration**

## ⚠️ Avoid These Tactics (Black Hat SEO)

❌ **Paid link schemes** - Against Google ToS
❌ **Link exchanges** - Low value and suspicious
❌ **PBN (Private Blog Networks)** - Risky, can result in penalties
❌ **Forum/Blog comment links** - Low quality, spammy
❌ **Directory stuffing** - Dilutes link profile
❌ **Irrelevant links** - More harm than good

✅ **FOCUS ON: Relevant, Editorial, Earned Links**
