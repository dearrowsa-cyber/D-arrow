## 🎓 Key Learnings

### What Works for D Arrow
1. **Local + Service Keywords** - High intent, lower competition
2. **Question-Based Keywords** - "How to...", "Best..."
3. **Location + Service** - "[City] digital marketing"
4. **Free/Consultation Keywords** - Strong CTA potential

### What to Focus On
1. **Content Quality** - 500+ words beats 200 words every time
2. **Mobile Experience** - 80% of traffic is mobile
3. **User Experience** - Fast, clear, easy to use pages rank better
4. **Consistency** - Regular updates signal active website

### What to Avoid
1. Keyword stuffing (>2% density is bad)
2. Duplicate content across pages
3. Ignoring mobile optimization
4. Neglecting user experience
5. Over-relying on backlinks alone
